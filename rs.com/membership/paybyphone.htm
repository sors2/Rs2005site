Subscribing to Runescape by Phone is quick and simple. How it works:

You dial a special number, the cost of the call* pays fort subscription.
You are told a unique PIN number - this is linked to your phone number.
You submit your phone number and PIN and your account is credited!
No more than 3 subscriptions are allowed on any one phone line at any time.**

Before dialing, please enter the phone number that you are planning to make the call from. You will then be guided through the call procedure.
